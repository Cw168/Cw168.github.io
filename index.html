Spruce trees

____

Mature white spruce trees can grow up to 30 m tall, depending on growing conditions.
The trunk of the white spruce usually measures between 0.3 and 0.6 m in diameter.
Habitat and Range: The white spruce tree has one of the largest ranges of any conifer and is one of the most northerly trees in North America.
